# Case Study: A4 Associates Transforms Project Management for NNPC

## The Challenge
A4 Associates Limited works as an engineering and project management consultant for the Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC). Managing high-pressure government-owned projects with multiple contractors demanded extreme accuracy, agility, and cost-effectiveness. The A4 Associates team needed to eliminate loose ends and promptly keep their principal informed of all developments. Previously, they relied on field agents to track project progress manually.

## The Solution: Microsoft Power Apps
Ha-Shem built a customized Enterprise Management Application using the Microsoft Power Platform to replace manual workflows:
* **Mobile Canvas Application**: Optimized for field agents to record real-time site progress, capture pictures, and complete milestone checklists.
* **Model-Driven Application**: Built for on-site project managers to receive, review, and track uploaded field updates.
* **Microsoft Dataverse**: Used as the central repository to securely store all project data.
* **Power BI Dashboard**: Integrated directly with the model-driven application to provide the NNPC team with a visually driven, holistic view of all project metrics.

## Business Results
* **Streamlined Communication**: The deployment established a more efficient project and report management process across all stakeholders.
* **Data-Driven Insights**: The integrated Power BI reporting provided robust capabilities, giving the client deeper visibility into project progress.
* **Digital Transformation**: The solution successfully met strict public-eye scrutiny and cost-effectiveness constraints.
