## Adobe Firefly Evolution – It Just Gets Better
Remember when Adobe Firefly first launched with AI image generation? It’s grown up. Now it can:
* Add advanced video tools
* Improve motion fidelity
* Sound Effects Generation
Why It Matters
For businesses, this means:
* A social media team
* An SME owner
* A training department
Firefly is lowering the barrier to high-quality content production, making it possible for small and mid-sized businesses to compete with larger organizations.
## Photoshop – Everywhere You Go
Adobe knows people don’t just work on desktops alone. With that, Photoshop now works seamlessly across desktop, web, and mobile.
Why It Matters
Say you start designing a product mockup at your desk, but your client calls you while you’re in traffic. Instead of saying, “I’ll fix it when I get back,” you can open Photoshop on your phone, make the tweak, and send it – instantly.
That’s not convenience alone. That’s a competitive advantage.
