# Ha-Shem Limited Becomes Certified to Deliver SolarWinds Certification Training

**Date:** June 30, 2026

**Author:** Queen Odili

## Ha-Shem Limited Becomes Certified to Deliver SolarWinds Certification Training

Ha-Shem Limited is now authorised to deliver official SolarWinds training, following the certification of our Technical Manager, Titilope Bello, as a SolarWinds Certified Instructor (SCI) (Observability Self-Hosted Track), SolarWinds Certified Professional (SCP).

The SCI programme is designed for experienced technical professionals and partners. It enables certified instructors to deliver SolarWinds training using licensed course materials and approved learning frameworks, with a clear standard for both technical depth and instructional delivery.

As an Advanced SolarWinds Partner, this places Ha-Shem Limited among the organisations now positioned to deliver official SolarWinds training within the partner ecosystem.

# What This Means

The SCI certification is awarded through a structured process that evaluates product knowledge, hands-on experience, and the ability to deliver training sessions to a defined standard.

Certified instructors are trained on how to deliver official course content, including guided instruction and lab-based sessions.

# Our Technical Expertise

In addition to the instructor certification, our team already holds multiple SolarWinds Certified Professional (SCP).

These certifications are part of the SCI eligibility requirements and are based on demonstrated knowledge of SolarWinds products.

They also require experience working with the platform across deployment, configuration, and system management.

This background shapes how training is delivered.

Sessions are informed by implementation experience, common configuration issues, and the kinds of challenges teams encounter during ongoing use.

# Why This Matters to Your Organisation

For organisations using SolarWinds Observability, the effectiveness of training has a direct impact on how well teams are able to work with the platform.

When training is structured and aligned with actual usage, IT teams are better positioned to:

- Configure systems in line with operational requirements
- Interpret system data and alerts with greater accuracy
- Identify and resolve issues without extended delays

The SCI programme supports this by combining official course materials with guided labs and defined learning paths that build capability step by step.

# What You Gain

Learning with Ha-Shem Limited, you have access to:

- Official SolarWinds course materials, including lab environments
- Training delivered by a certified instructor with validated product experience
- Programmes aligned with SCP certification pathways
- Local delivery options, with flexibility to fit your schedule

This supports both onboarding for new teams and continued development for teams already using the platform.

This now extends that support into skills development and training.
