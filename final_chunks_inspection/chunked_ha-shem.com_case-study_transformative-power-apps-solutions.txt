### Axella Cuts Training Costs & Onboarding Time by 60%

Our biggest challenge was streamlining training and onboarding. Ha-Shem developed a training management app that integrates seamlessly with SharePoint and Power Automate. It eliminated errors and saves our HR team a whopping **60%** of their time in planning and approving budgets!

We recently deployed two applications for Axxela, a major player in the Oil and Gas sector. The first is a Training Management Application that eliminates time wastage and human errors associated with manual management processes.

=========================================

Our solution pulls data from Axxela’s SharePoint database, creating an algorithm that accesses staff and division information and distributes budgets equitably. The solution also taps into capabilities of Power Automate to create an approval workflow. This solution has eliminated human error and saved the Human Resources team **60%** of the time previously spent planning and managing annual staff training and budget approval. It also keeps their training record to ensure they meet regulatory requirements.
